Why do i like detective conan?

Detective Conan is a favorite because of its intricate mysteries and engaging characters. The series offers a blend of action, suspense, and clever gadgets that make each episode thrilling. Fans appreciate the educational aspect, learning about forensic science and problem-solving. Its long-running nature provides consistency and nostalgia, while also offering cultural insights into Japanese life.
